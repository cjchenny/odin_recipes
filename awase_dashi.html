Dashi is the basic Japanese soup stock used in many Japanese dishes. Learn how to make Awase Dashi at home with umami-packed ingredients like kombu (dried kelp) and katsuobushi (dried bonito flakes).

Ingredients

  • 1 piece kombu(dried kelp)
  • katsuobushi(dried bonito flakes
  • 4 cups of water

Instructions

  1. gather all ingredients
  2. make several slits in kombu
  3. Add the kombu and water to a medium saucepan.
  4. Turn on the heat to medium low and slowly bring to almost boiling, about 10 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, clean the dashi by skimming the foam and debris from the surface.
  6. Just befor the dashi starts boiling gently, remove the kombu from the saucepan
  7. Add 1 cup katsuovushi and bring it back to a boil again
  8. Once the dashi is boiling, reduce the geat, simmer for just 30 seconds, and turn off the heat.
  9. Let the katsuobushi sink to the bottom, about 10 minutes.
  10. Strain the dashi through a finemesh strainer
