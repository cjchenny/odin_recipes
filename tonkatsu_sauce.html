Tonkatsu Sauce

Tonkatsu sauce (とんかつソース) is a popular Japanese condiment that’s traditionally made from fruits and vegetables like tomatoes, prunes, and apples, plus soy sauce, vinegar, and Asian spices.

Ingredients

  • worcestershire sauce
  • sugar
  • ketchup
  • oyster sauce

Intructions

  1. dissolve the sugar in the worcestershire sauce
  2. add the ketchup and oyster sauce
